Welcome to FuelMyRun.

I'm a semi-healthy eating, beer loving, runner.

This concept started as an Instagram page, and has now become a blog (we'll see how that goes...) I've always liked making nutrient-dense, delicious meals, and one day I decided to take a picture. I didn't want to be obnoxious on my personal Instagram, so I decided to create another page to post as many pics as I wanted. As a marathon runners, these meals literally "fuel my run"s, so I thought I would share recipes I love and/or craving with others who may be looking for inspiration.
